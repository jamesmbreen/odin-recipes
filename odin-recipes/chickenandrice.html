James Hamburger!

Description

Pan-fred and then baked chicken with rice-a-roni chicken flavored rice

Ingrdients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Rice-a-roni chicken rice
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Oil
  • flour

Steps:

Chicken

  1. Pound the chicken to about 1/8th to 1/4" thickness
  2. Season generously with salt and pepper
  3. Cover chicken with flour, dip in egg bath, then cover in bread crumbs
  4. Heat oil in skillet, once hot, fry chicken on both sides until brown. Work in batches.
  5. Transfer chicken to 350 degree oven for 15 minutes
