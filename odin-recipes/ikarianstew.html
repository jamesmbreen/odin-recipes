Ikarian Stew

Description

An amazing stew based on the diet of the people from Ikaria.

Ingredients:

  • 8oz can of black-eyed peas
  • 1/2cup EVOO
  • 1 large red onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 fennel bulb
  • 1 large ripe tomato
  • 2 tsp tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 bunch of dill chopped
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat and cook onion, garlic, and fennel
  2. Add black-eyed peas and stir
  3. Add tomato, tomato paste, and water to cover beans by 1"
  4. Add bay leaves
  5. Bring pot to a boil and simmer 10 minutes
  6. Add chopped dill and salt to taste
  7. Cook another 20 minutes
  8. Remove from heat and discard bay leaves
